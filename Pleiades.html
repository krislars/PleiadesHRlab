Pleiades Star Cluster

HR Diagram Lab by Kristen Larson (Western Washington University)

Hover over a star and record the temperature and luminosity shown here:
 
Or, click on a star and scroll to the bottom of the data popup to find temperature (Teff, in Kelvin) and luminosity (Lbol, in solar units).



Reference:


Data are from the paper The Seven Sisters DANCe by Bouy et al. (2015) Objects shown belong to the star cluster with 95 percent probability. Green symbols are from the second release of the DANCe-Pleiades survey, and blue symbols are from the Tycho-2 catalog. There are many published studies of the Pleiades star cluster, but we chose this one because it includes temperature and luminosity in solar units in the published catalog available online. Many thanks to Bouy et al!
